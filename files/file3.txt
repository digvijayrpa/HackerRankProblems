Once upon a time, ek chhoti si town mein ek ladki rehti thi jiska naam tha Pari. Pari thi sabse sundar, pyaari, aur sabke dil ki rani. Uske sundar smile aur pyari baatein sabko khush kar deti thi. Ek din, jab Pari apne favourite garden mein phoolon ke beech baithi thi, tabhi ek chamatkari cheel uske paas aayi.
Woh cheel Pari se boli, "Pari, tum itni pyaari ho ki tumhe ek special gift milna chahiye. Main tumhe ek magical key deti hoon jo tumhare har sapne ko poora kar sakti hai." Pari ne hairani se cheel se woh key le li aur socha, "Yeh kaise kaam karegi?"
Us raat, Pari ne sochte hue apni aankhein band kar li aur sochne lagi ki kya wish kare. Tabhi usne socha, "Main ek aisa dost chahti hoon jo hamesha mere saath ho, mere saare raaz samjhe aur kabhi mujhe akela feel na hone de." Jaise hi usne yeh socha, woh key chamak uthi aur Pari ke samne ek pyara sa ladka aa gaya jiska naam tha Arjun.
Arjun aur Pari jaldi se ache dost ban gaye. Woh dono mil kar har din naye adventures pe jate, kabhi jungle mein khojte, kabhi pahaadon pe chadhte, aur kabhi nadi mein tairte. Arjun hamesha Pari ka dhyan rakhta aur uske saath rehta, chahe kitna bhi mushkil kyun na ho. Aur Pari ne bhi apni magical key se Arjun ke liye har wo cheez mangayi jo usse khush kar sake.
Dheere dheere, Arjun aur Pari ki dosti aur gehri hoti gayi aur woh ek dusre ke bina apne din ki kalpana bhi nahi kar sakte the. Ek din, Arjun ne Pari se kaha, "Pari, tumne mujhe woh sab kuch diya hai jo main kabhi soch bhi nahi sakta tha. Tum meri sabse badi wish ho." Pari ki aankhon mein aansu aa gaye aur usne kaha, "Aur tum mere liye sabse pyaara gift ho, Arjun."
Aur is tarah, Pari aur Arjun hamesha khush rahe, naye naye sapne dekhte aur unhe mil kar poora karte rahe. Aur Pari ko pata tha ki uski magical key se sabse badi wish poori ho chuki thi - ek pyaara dost jo hamesha uske saath rahega.
Goodnight, Parul. Sweet dreams
